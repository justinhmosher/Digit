About Dyne

The Country Club Experience

For restaurants: more tables turned, more tips, and more regulars. For guests: no check at the end of the night.

What we do

Dyne connects restaurants and guests through a simple, browser-based checkout. No app download. No waiting for a check. Just tap, pay, and go.

Behind the scenes, Dyne quietly captures the signals that matter: which dishes guests crave, which experiences feel special, and which visits turn into regulars.

The result is a smoother night for staff, a better experience for guests, and a smarter playbook for owners.

Why restaurants choose Dyne

  • Guests close out from their phone, without interrupting the floor.
  • Owners see which menu items and experiences lead to repeat visits.
  • Service feels more like a country club: no running checks, no chasing cards.

For restaurants

Dyne is built for operators who care about hospitality and growth. We remove friction at checkout and give you clarity on what’s working in the room.

Understand which dishes keep guests coming back, how different shifts perform, and what your customers are saying so you can refine what matters most. Use Dyne to improve service, coach staff, and design experiences that feel intentionally crafted.

All of this happens without changing your core service style— Dyne plugs in quietly and lets your team do what they do best.

For guests

Guests get the experience they already want: arrive, enjoy, and leave on their own terms—without the awkward “check dance.”

Dyne lets people review their live receipt, tip confidently, and walk out when they’re ready. Their loyalty is tied to the restaurants they love, not another app on their phone.

And when guests share feedback, it goes straight to the teams that can act on it—helping elevate each return visit.

How Dyne works for your guests

From the guest’s perspective, Dyne is simple. There’s no app to download and nothing new for them to learn—just a smoother way to pay and leave when they’re ready.

1

Share their member number

At the start of the meal, the guest gives their Dyne member number to the server. You ring in the check as normal—Dyne simply links that ticket to their profile.

2

View a live receipt

As items are added, the guest can pull up a live receipt on their phone—seeing every course, modifier, and tax line in real time. No surprises at the end of the night.

3

Pay & tip from the table

When they’re ready, guests pay directly from their phone using a secure, Stripe-powered checkout. They choose their tip, confirm, and the check is closed—no handing over cards or waiting for signatures.

4

Leave at their convenience

Once payment is complete, guests are free to leave when it feels natural to them. They can optionally leave quick feedback on their experience, giving you signal on what brings them back.

For your team, this means fewer trips to the terminal and less time chasing checks, thereby creating quicker table turnover and more revenue— while Dyne quietly captures the data behind each visit.
