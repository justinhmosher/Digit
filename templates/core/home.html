How Dyne improves your restaurant’s revenue

Dyne is not “another app” — it’s a way to get more value from every guest that walks through your doors, and to turn them into regulars.

1

New guests from the Dyne Network

You’re not starting from zero. Dyne brings a pool of members who are actively looking for new places to eat and see “New to Dyne” restaurants first.

2

Clarity on what actually drives repeat visits

Dyne ties together checks, dishes, staff, and member IDs. You see which menu items, sections, and servers lead to higher return rates, so you can make decisions with data — not guesses.

3

Country club–style checkout that keeps the room moving

Guests pay in seconds and leave when they’re ready. Your staff isn’t stuck running cards, splitting checks, or printing receipts — they’re opening new tabs and turning more seats.
